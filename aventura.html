Port Aventura World

Port Aventura World is actually 2 parks (Port Aventura Park and Ferrari Land) Port Aventura Park has 7 fairly quality roller coasters (and one kids coaster,) while Ferrari Land has Europe's tallest roller coaster. Entry to the main park is 45 dollars and you can also enter Ferrari Land for an extra 7 dollars (or enter just Ferrari Land for 21 dollars.) Port Aventura World is notoriou for intentionally slowly operating their rides, and also having ridiculous amounts of people who payed for their express pass be able to board the ride, which effectively forces you to pay a minimum of 31 dollars for the lowest tier. Port Aventura world is located in Salou (67 miles southwest of Barcelona.) It looks nice and is definitely worth visiting, but is intended to cost a lot of money.

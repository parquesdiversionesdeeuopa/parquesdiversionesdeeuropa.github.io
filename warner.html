Parque Warner Madrid

Parque Warner Madrid is certainley the better park in Madrid as it has 4 quality roller coasters (1 kids coaster, one more family oriented coaster, and one bad roller coaster.) The park looks nice, escpecially the image shown above if it was higher quality, admission is 30 dollars. It's located outside Madrid and if we fly into or train through Madrid it's a good option as it's problably the best theme park in Spain.
